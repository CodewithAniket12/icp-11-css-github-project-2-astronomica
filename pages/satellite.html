Types of Satellites

Communication Satellites

Used for transmitting television, radio, internet, and military communications.

Earth Observation Satellites

Monitor environmental changes, weather patterns, and disaster management.

Navigation Satellites

Provide global positioning services for navigation and geolocation.

Reconnaissance Satellites

Used for military surveillance and intelligence gathering.

Space Telescopes

Observe distant celestial objects beyond Earth's atmosphere.
